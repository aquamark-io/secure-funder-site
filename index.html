About This Program

The Secure Funder™ certification program is administered by Aquamark, an independent third party specializing in document security and data protection controls within the alternative finance industry. Certification is based on a technical assessment process evaluating how sensitive merchant information is handled within participating organizations, including the identification and remediation of potential vulnerabilities where data could be improperly accessed, shared, or exfiltrated.
