About This Program

The Secure Funder™ certification program is administered by Aquamark, an independent third party specializing in document security and data-protection controls within the alternative finance industry. Certification is based on a technical verification process evaluating how sensitive information is handled within participating organizations, including the identification of potential vulnerabilities where data could be improperly accessed, shared, or exfiltrated. For inquiries regarding the certification program, please contact info@aquamark.io
