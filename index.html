About

Why Secure Funder Exists

Alternative finance has grown rapidly, but unlike traditional banking, there's no regulatory body overseeing how organizations handle sensitive information. As a result, inconsistent practices, mishandling, and misuse of merchant data have become a systemic issue across the industry.

Secure Funder exists to provide a clear, independent way for organizations to demonstrate responsible data stewardship and leadership. Through transparent requirements and third-party verification, the program allows firms with strong practices to make that commitment visible — and distinguish themselves from practices that undermine trust across the industry.
