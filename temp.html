Establishing Industry Standards

Data-Handling Standards for Alternative Finance

Secure Funder™ is an independent certification body establishing baseline safeguards for how organizations handle sensitive merchant information. Voluntary membership. Independent verification. Publicly listed.

Our mission is to promote responsible data handling across the alternative finance industry by establishing practical, independently verified standards that protect merchants, partners, and the organizations that serve them.

About the Program

Why Secure Funder Exists

There is no shared standard for how organizations in alternative finance handle the sensitive merchant information they receive — whether through broker submissions, direct applications, or third-party channels. Bank statements, tax returns, and financial documents move between parties daily, often without basic safeguards governing who can access them, how they're stored, or what happens when they leave the building.

This creates real risk. Deals get stolen. Merchant data gets exposed. Trust breaks down between partners, and merchants bear the consequences. The industry has grown rapidly, but unlike traditional banking, it's largely unregulated — which means the industry itself needs to define what responsible looks like.

Secure Funder was created to fill that gap — not through regulation or mandates, but through a voluntary program that gives organizations a clear framework for responsible data handling and a way to demonstrate that commitment publicly.

Guiding Principles

Protect Merchants

Merchant data deserves the same care as financial assets. Participation signals that protection is a priority.

Transparency

Requirements are public. Verification is independent. Certified members are listed for anyone to see.

Industry-Led

Standards are shaped by the organizations that participate. Members help define what responsible looks like.

Practical Standards

Requirements reflect how organizations actually operate — not theoretical ideals. Equivalent controls are accepted.

The industry is growing. Its standards should grow with it.

Secure Funder helps organizations demonstrate the safeguards that merchants and partners are starting to expect.

Certification Standards

What Verification Covers

Certification evaluates how organizations handle sensitive merchant information across six areas. Requirements are public, and equivalent controls are accepted.

Email Security

How inbound merchant documents are received and processed. MFA requirements, automated handling protocols, and access restrictions on sensitive attachments.

CRM & Portal Security

Controls governing system access — multi-factor authentication, role-based permissions, user verification, and document accessibility settings.

Cloud Storage & Third Parties

How documents are stored outside core systems, including third-party access controls, monitoring, and data governance practices.

Outsourcing

Whether BPO teams or offshore staff have access to merchant documents, and what safeguards exist when third parties handle information on your behalf.

Personnel Controls

Background checks, password policies, screen security, off-boarding processes — the human-level protections that safeguard data at the front line.

Culture & Leadership

Whether data protection is treated as an operational priority — communicated internally, reinforced by leadership, and embedded in organizational culture.

Membership

How to Join

Membership is open to any funding organization in alternative finance. The process is designed to be straightforward — most organizations already have many of the required safeguards in place.

1

Apply

Submit your organization's information. We'll confirm eligibility and provide an overview of the verification process and requirements.

2

Prepare

Review the requirements and implement any remaining safeguards. Organizations enrolled as "In Progress" have 90 days to complete verification. Extensions are available under certain circumstances.

3

Verify

Complete a brief screen share review conducted by an independent verifier. All information reviewed during verification is treated as confidential.

4

Get Listed

Certified members are listed in the public directory with a digital badge for use on websites, email signatures, and marketing materials.
