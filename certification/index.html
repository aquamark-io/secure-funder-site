Overview

Secure Funder™ verifies that your organization has technical safeguards in place to prevent unauthorized use of merchant information. Verification is conducted on-site or via screenshare, where you demonstrate your controls in real time.

Upon successful completion, you'll receive your official certification badge and listing in our public directory, where brokers and business owners can identify organizations committed to data protection. Where a specific safeguard is not in place, functionally equivalent or compensating controls may be accepted.

1

Email Security

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled

Submission Handling

IF broker submissions are accepted via email, THEN one of the following is required:
Option A: Automated email processing with restricted human access
Submissions automatically pushed to CRM without human intervention
Only management may access submission inbox for configuration/troubleshooting purposes
Processing personnel cannot access the submission inbox
Option B: Use the Secure Funder Gateway when email submissions require human handling
No changes to broker submission inbox or workflow
When the broker submits, attachments are automatically routed through gateway to watermark files and embed metadata
Files are then sent to inbox of your choice for human handling (e.g., processing@yourcompany.com)
Access Gateway
2

CRM Security

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled

Access Controls

Role-based access:
Personnel can only access accounts and information required for their role
Sensitive fields (e.g., SSN) are accessible based on role requirements and business necessity
Recommended: For added security, enable screenshot restrictions or screen watermarking.
User verification:
Users must be from your domain, with exception of service-level access (developers, vendors)
IF documents are stored in your CRM, THEN choose one:
Option A: View-only access to files (no download capability)
Option B: Files are watermarked before entering CRM or at time of download
Any document watermarking solution is acceptable
IF full database download capability exists, THEN:
This function must be restricted to senior personnel only
3

Portal Security

Note: If you do not have a portal, skip this section.

Access Controls

IF sensitive information is accessible in your portal (e.g. SSN), THEN:
Access is restricted based on role requirements and business necessity
IF files are available in your portal, THEN choose one:
Option A: View-only access to files (no download capability)
Option B: Files are watermarked before entering portal or at time of download
Any document watermarking solution is acceptable
4

BPO Access

Note: If you do not use BPO teams (on-shore or off-shore), skip this section.

On-Shore / Off-Shore Teams

IF BPO teams have access to merchant documents, THEN choose one:
Option A: View-only access to files (no download capability)
Option B: Documents are watermarked before third-party access
Any document watermarking solution is acceptable
5

Cloud Storage

Cloud Service Usage

IF you use cloud storage services (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) to house merchant documents, THEN:
Users have view-only access OR documents are watermarked if downloadable
Any document watermarking solution is acceptable
6

Personnel Controls

Background Checks

Personnel must pass background checks appropriate for their role

Password Policy

Documented requirements including minimum complexity and periodic changes View sample policy

Screen Security

Screen lock required when away from workstation

Workstation Policy

Personal cell phones may not be used at workstations where customer data is visible

Off-boarding Process

Documented process for immediately revoking system access when employees leave or change roles
