Certification Requirements

A structured, lightweight way to demonstrate responsible handling of sensitive information.

Important note: These requirements reflect the standards we verify, but we recognize that every organization is different. If you have equivalent controls that provide the same level of protection, we're happy to review them. The objective is safeguarding submissions — not rigid compliance.

If you're unsure whether your current setup meets a requirement, contact us with the details and a team member will respond within 24 hours.

Email Security

Prevent access to unprotected submissions.

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled.

IF broker submissions are accepted via email, THEN one of the following is required:
Option A: Automated email processing with restricted human access
  • Submissions pushed to CRM without human intervention
  • Only management may access submission inbox, not processing personnel
Option B: Use document protection if submissions require human handling
  • No changes for your partners — continue emailing deals as usual
  • Inbound email triggers webhook to watermark attachments
  • Processors work exclusively from protected files, instantly reducing risk.

CRM / Portal Security

Ensuring your systems limit access to what's needed.

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled.

Role-Based Access

Personnel can only access accounts and information required for their role. Sensitive fields like SSN are restricted based on business necessity.

User Verification

Users must be from your domain, with exception for service-level access (developers, vendors).

IF documents are accessible THEN
Option A: View-only access to files (no download capability)
Option B: Files are watermarked before entering the system, or at time of download

Any document watermarking solution is acceptable.

How It Works →
Note: If full database download exists, it must be restricted to senior personnel only.

Document Storage

Understanding how documents are protected outside your core systems.

If storage documents in other locations (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc), we'd like to understand what roles have access, what kind of access, and how that side is kept secure or is being monitored.

Outsourcing

If you use BPO teams (on-shore or off-shore) with access to merchant documents.

IF BPO teams have access to submission documents THEN
Option A: View-only access to files (no download capability)
Option B: Documents are watermarked before third-party access

Any document watermarking solution is acceptable.

Personnel Controls

Standard policies to protect data at the human level.

For these items, provide copies of your documented policies. Redactions to protect proprietary information are acceptable.

Background Checks

Personnel must pass background checks appropriate for their role.

Password Policy

Documented requirements including minimum complexity and periodic changes.

Screen Security

Screen lock required when away from workstation. Personal cell phones may not be used at workstations where customer data is visible.

Off-boarding Process

Documented process for immediately revoking system access when employees leave or change roles.

Culture & Leadership

Setting the tone from the top.

Consider communicating your Secure Funder™ certification internally. Leadership visibility around security standards reinforces expectations and underscores your commitment to industry-wide best practices. Whether incorporated into onboarding, team meetings, or internal communications, it signals that protecting sensitive information is a core operational priority — not just a compliance exercise.

Terms and Conditions

The Secure Funder™ certification program establishes an independent standard for identifying trusted funders in the alternative finance industry. Certification is based exclusively on verifiable safeguards that are applied consistently across all participants. Equivalent controls may be accepted when they meet the intent of the requirements and can be independently verified during review.

Enrollment in the program represents a voluntary commitment to elevated standards of data security and responsible business practices. Secure Funder™ is designed for funders of all sizes and focuses narrowly on protecting submissions and the documents associated with them.

The Secure Funder™ Directory opens April 1, 2026. Organizations that enroll prior to this date will be recognized as Charter Members.

Safeguards are not required to be in place at the time of enrollment, but must be fully implemented prior to verification. Verification must be completed within ninety (90) days of enrollment. Listings will be removed if verification is not scheduled within this period, though extensions may be granted under special circumstances.

Verification is conducted via live screen share. All information reviewed during verification is treated as confidential. Participants should review How Verification Works in advance of their session.

While no program can prevent theft entirely, Secure Funder™ is designed to materially reduce exposure and deter misuse through practical, verifiable safeguards, without introducing unnecessary operational disruption.

The annual program fee is $2,499 USD. This fee covers verification, program administration, ongoing Directory placement, and supports Secure Funder's role as a visible sponsor at major industry conferences — extending recognition and visibility for participating funders. For organizations using Aquamark tools such as watermarking (not required for certification), the annual fee is waived. No payment is due until verification is scheduled. Monthly payment arrangements are available upon request.

Participants agree to promptly notify Secure Funder™ of any material changes that may affect certification status. Certification may be revoked if required safeguards are no longer maintained or if information provided during verification is found to be inaccurate.

Digital certification badges are issued upon successful verification and may be displayed on company websites and materials. Badges remain valid for the duration of active membership.
