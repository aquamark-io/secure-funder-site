Certification Requirements

Baseline standards designed to be lightweight and practical.

Equivalent controls are accepted when they meet the intent of a requirement and can be verified during review. If you're unsure whether your current setup qualifies, contact us — a team member will respond within one business day.

Email Security

Prevent unauthorized access to unprotected submissions.

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled.

IF broker submissions are accepted via email THEN one of the following is required:
Option A: Automated Processing Submissions are pushed to CRM without human intervention. Only management may access the submission inbox directly.
Option B: Document Protection Inbound submissions trigger watermarking before human handling. No changes required for your partners — deals continue by email as usual.

Any document watermarking solution is acceptable.

CRM / Portal Security

System-level controls to ensure access is limited to what's needed.

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA must be enabled on CRM and portal accounts.

Role-Based Access

Personnel can only access accounts and information required for their role. Sensitive fields (e.g. SSN) are restricted based on business necessity.

User Verification

CRM users must be from your domain, with exception for service-level access (developers, vendors).

IF documents are accessible within your CRM THEN
Option A: View-Only File access restricted to view-only — no download capability.
Option B: Watermarked Files Files are watermarked before entering the system, or at time of download.

Any document watermarking solution is acceptable.

Personnel Controls

Documented policies to protect data at the human level. Provide copies of existing policies — redactions to protect proprietary information are acceptable.

Background Checks

Personnel must pass background checks appropriate for their role.

Password Policy

Documented requirements including minimum complexity and periodic changes.

Screen Security

Screen lock required when away from workstation. Personal cell phones may not be used at workstations where customer data is visible.

Off-boarding Process

Documented process for immediately revoking system access when employees leave or change roles.

Culture & Leadership

Setting the tone from the top.

We encourage communicating your Secure Funder™ certification internally — whether through onboarding, team meetings, or internal communications. Leadership visibility around security standards reinforces expectations and signals that protecting sensitive information is an operational priority, not just a compliance exercise.

Program Terms

Secure Funder™ is an independent certification program for the alternative finance industry. Certification is based on verifiable safeguards applied consistently across all participants. Equivalent controls may be accepted when they meet the intent of a requirement and can be independently verified.

Enrollment is voluntary. The program is designed for funders of all sizes and focuses on protecting submissions and the documents associated with them. The Secure Funder™ Directory opens April 1, 2026. Organizations that enroll prior to this date are recognized as Charter Members.

Safeguards are not required to be in place at enrollment, but must be fully implemented prior to verification. Verification must be completed within 90 days of enrollment. Listings will be removed if verification is not scheduled within this period, though extensions may be granted. Verification is conducted via live screen share; all information reviewed is treated as confidential.

The annual program fee is $2,499 USD. No payment is due until verification is scheduled. Monthly installments are available. For organizations using Aquamark tools such as watermarking (not required for certification), the annual fee is waived.

Participants agree to notify Secure Funder™ of material changes that may affect certification status. Certification may be revoked if required safeguards are no longer maintained or if information provided during verification is found to be inaccurate. Digital certification badges are issued upon verification and remain valid for the duration of active membership.
