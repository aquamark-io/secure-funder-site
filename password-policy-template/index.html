Password Policy

Purpose

This policy establishes minimum requirements for password creation and management to protect company systems and sensitive financial data.

Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to company systems, applications, or data.

Requirements

Password Strength

  • Minimum 12 characters
  • Must include: uppercase letter, lowercase letter, number, and special character
  • Cannot contain username, company name, or common words

Password Management

  • Change passwords every 90 days
  • Never share passwords with anyone

Account Security

  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all systems that support it
  • Lock workstation when leaving desk
  • Report compromised or suspected compromised passwords to IT immediately

Prohibited Practices

  • Writing passwords on paper or storing in unsecured digital files
  • Sharing accounts or credentials
  • Saving passwords in browsers on shared devices

Enforcement

Violations may result in account suspension and disciplinary action up to termination.
