About This Directory

Secure Funder™ is intentionally narrow in scope: it focuses on safeguards that reduce unauthorized access to submissions and the documents associated with them. It is not positioned as a comprehensive security audit.

Public resource

What the public should know

Directory placement reflects the organization’s participation and status in the program at the time of publication. Verification is performed through practical review of safeguards. No program can prevent all misuse; the objective is to materially reduce exposure and deter common misuse through verifiable controls.

Questions or corrections can be directed to info@aquamark.io.
